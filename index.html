The Man behind the screen!

Xavier Proctor was born in the wild and wonderful state of West Virginia. He moved to the great state of Maryland during his childhood years.

Xavier had been a football player for most of his life and even played professional football for 4 years. After his career as an professional athlete he became a coach and a high school teacher. He loves to teach but his passion is in technology and coding. In 2017 he took the plunge into his new journey and enrolled in the UNC Chapel Hill Coding Boot Camp for full stack developers. He will graduate from the boot camp on November 4th, 2017. He is very excited about his new career in coding and is looking forward to learning more everyday.

He played professionall football for the Orlando Predators(AFL), Detroit Lions(NFL), New York Giants(NFL) and Hamilton Tigercats(CFL).
